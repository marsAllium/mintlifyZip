We support on demand solana schemas for the following raw and enriched entities. If your team requires realtime and historical data since genesis for a specific set of wallets, additional parsing (such as by dex trades, nft trades, etc.) or additional enrichment (such as with live pricing information and token metadata), please contact our team at eng\@allium.so to explore solutions.

| Description          | Tables                                                                            |
| -------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Raw blockchain data. | blocks, transactions, instructions, inner\_instructions, pubkey\_maps             |
| Enriched Data.       | token\_transfers, assets\_fungible\_balances\_latest, assets\_enriched\_transfers |
