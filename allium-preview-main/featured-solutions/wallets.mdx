title: "Wallets"
---

Wallets providers use [Developer](https://docs.allium.so/products/allium-developer) and [Datastreams](https://docs.allium.so/products/allium-datastreams) to power their UX and backends with real-time [enriched transaction data](https://docs.allium.so/products/allium-developer/wallet-apis) and [block-level wallet balances](https://docs.allium.so/products/allium-developer/wallet-apis). Wallets [benefit](https://www.allium.so/post/the-inside-story-of-the-jup-airdrop-at-phantom) from Allium's horizontally scalable infrastructure for consistent stability throughout peak network traffic (up to 100K+ QPS).

Wallet analysts use [Explorer](https://docs.allium.so/products/allium-explorer) and [Datashares](https://docs.allium.so/products/allium-datashares) to source user behavior insights and ecosystem intelligence through enriched schemas like [Wallet360](https://docs.allium.so/historical-data-schema-view/wallet-360).
