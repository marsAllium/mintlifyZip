---
title: "DEXs"
---

DEX providers use Allium to power front-end UX & customer-facing analytics, analyze protocol adoption & market positioning, and automate finance workflows. DEXs benefit from Allium's enriched schemas, including tailored DEX schemas and Wallet360, which enables immediate visibility into user insights, market intelligence, and protocol performance.

DEX compliance teams use Allium to protect token distributions and liquidity events from exploitation. By enabling identification of suspicious patterns across thousands of wallets and transactions, Allium helps DEX protocols maintain integrity during high-stakes events like airdrops, ensuring rewards reach genuine community members rather than sybil attackers.
