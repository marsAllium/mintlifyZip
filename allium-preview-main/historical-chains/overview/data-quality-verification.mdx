## Data quality checks

We believe in full transparency to our users on the completness and veracity of our data. Allium runs regular data status checks ([https://status.allium.so/](https://status.allium.so/)) and ([https://app.allium.so/status/chains](https://app.allium.so/status/chains))

Check the completness of the data in realtime on our status page

* **Existence Checks**: Ensuring that every block, trace, log, transaction exists on the fundamental raw data level

* **Entity Consistency Checks**: Ensuring the blocks are in order, the number of transactions match

* **Sub-entity Consistency Checks:&#x20;**&#x45;nsuring the internal fields are correct as RPCs can flake especially during versin upgrades
