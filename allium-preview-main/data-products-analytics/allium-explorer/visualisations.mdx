### How to create visualisations

After running the query, toggle the "Chart" table below the query editor to open up the visualisation tool.

### Charting Tool

The visualisation feature allows you to create and share your data visualizations with a simple click of a button. Click on the "Chart" button and select the desired chart type from the dropdown menu.

Created your desired charts.

### Sharing Visual

The "Share Chart" feature allows you to share your charts, the results and the underlying SQL code to the public.

Click on the "Share Chart" button above the "Chart Setting" side bar on the right to share your visualisation.

![](https://docs.allium.so/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F137711553-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FUrfZbzSRStJdjOp5DS3Z%252Fuploads%252Fgit-blob-bdd4e339c21d087ed3754cd1e3f90d0aba330001%252Fquery_viz_share.png%3Falt%3Dmedia\&width=768\&dpr=4\&quality=100\&sign=1375a9fe\&sv=2)

Copy the share linked or embed depending on your use case

### Unpublishing a visualisation

If you would like to unpublish the share and query, click on the "Unpublish Link" button.
