**How to Use the Ingest Feature in Allium Explorer**

1. **Navigate to the Ingest Page**: Open Allium Explorer and go to the "Ingest" section from the main menu.

2. **Select Your Dataset**: Enter the desired table name and then click on the "Choose file" button and select your file from your local system. Accepted file formats are CSV currently.

3. **Upload**: Click "Upload" button to insert the dataset. The data will now be available within Allium Explorer for analysis and exploration.

![](https://docs.allium.so/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F137711553-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FUrfZbzSRStJdjOp5DS3Z%252Fuploads%252F641AabM5T5KsBLv2nuo3%252Fimage.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D84781c07-69fc-427c-b500-2b5163532ce2\&width=768\&dpr=4\&quality=100\&sign=a02be0d2\&sv=2)
