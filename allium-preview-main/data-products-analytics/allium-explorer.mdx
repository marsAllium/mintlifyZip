Allium App: Explorer Interface

**Allium Explorer** is a one-stop destination to **query, analyze,&#x20;**&#x61;n&#x64;**&#x20;visualize** our [Historical Data](https://docs.allium.so/historical-data/overview). Explorer is a web app that allows you to query, and visualize blockchain data across 75+ blockchains and share your insights through interactive charts.

### Query Engine

The Allium Explorer runs on **Snowflake Data Warehouse**.

### Schema Catalog

The Allium Explorer provides access to all 7**0+ blockchains** listed on this [page](/historical-chains/supported-blockchains).

### **Explorer Features**

The Allium Explorer supports the following features to power your analytic workflows.

[Query Runner](/data-products-analytics/allium-explorer/query-runner)

Query batch blockchain data indexed by Allium with SQL.

[Allium AI Assistant](/data-products-analytics/allium-explorer/allium-ai-assistant)

Generate SQL queries from plain English questions with the help of AI.

[Explorer API](/data-products-analytics/allium-explorer/explorer-api)

Convert your SQL queries into an API endpoint with a click of a button.

[Data Upload CSV + API](/data-products-analytics/allium-explorer/data-upload)

Upload your datasets to integrate into your queries.

[Visualisations](/data-products-analytics/allium-explorer/visualisations)

Craft and share data visualizations to present your insights.
